About Us









Mega-Transit And Logistics Company Ltd
Mega-Transit | Logistic | Company | Ltd is a logistics solutions provider based in Cameroon.
Made up of a team of experienced professionals in the fields of international logistics, transport and customs clearance of goods; Our activities also extend into trading mainly tropical timber species. All this in compliance with international standards. We position ourselves as a facilitator in international exchanges. The satisfaction of our customers remains at the heart of our concerns.
We provide tailor-made solutions tailored to the types of activities and needs of any individual and/or company involved in sea, land, and air imports and exports.
Thanks to the superior quality of our services, we aim to be a leader in the logistics and trading sector in the sub-region through exceptional customer service.
